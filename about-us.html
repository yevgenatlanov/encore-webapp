ABOUT US

A FAMILY OWNED AND OPERATED COMPANY SINCE 1981

BEHIND ENCORE HOSPITALITY CARPETS STANDS THE BRUMLOW FAMILY GUARANTEE

With roots in chenille bedspread manufacturing, we have made our living on servicing our customers to the highest degree possible. Our passion, no matter size of order or type of customer, is that everyone experiences the same level of purchasing carpet products from a textile mill that they can trust.

Since 1981, Brumlow has grown into a nationally recognized manufacturer in both the carpet and rug industries, citing multiple awards such as the JCPenny Supplier of the Year, Kohl’s Partners in Progress and Shopko Supplier of the Year. The combination of large company manufacturing processes and capabilities and a small company focus, we are able to o er superb attention to detail with a quality that is unsurpassed in the industry.

It is and always has been our goal to stay on the forefront of technology and by doing so, we have become one of the most innovative and forward-thinking manufacturers in the carpet industry today. Our small, family owned business, with all the manufacturing capabilities of a large corporation, allows our customers the ability to choose from a broad range of products while not sacrificing personalized services they deserve. We remove the “no” out of the equation and by being a vertically integrated factory, we are able to o er fast lead times, unlimited custom capabilities and outstanding customer service.

We are conveniently located in Calhoun, Georgia, in close proximity to suppliers in the industry allowing us to service your project as if it were our own!

Infinity

Known as the gold standard in guest rooms carpet, the Infinity product has proven itself as “the best there is”. Using Zeftron solution dyed nylon, as it’s name suggests the Infinity creates infinite amounts of pile heights and infinite design repeats at extremely fast speeds which allows Encore to service the guest room market quickly and competitively.

Learn more >

Colorpoint

Using Zeftron solution dyed nylon the Colorpoint creates “full gauge” density to create detailed patterns as opposed to “burying” ends which lowers the density and wear of the product. The use of up to 6 colors at “full gauge” makes Colorpoint an attractive choice for Public Space applications.

Learn more >

Enhanced Loop

Using Zeftron solution dyed nylon, the Enhanced Loop uses varying pile heights to create more traditional subtle textures with limited design repeats. As a very cost effective option the enhanced has been a constant in the hospitality marketplace for years

Learn more >

Colortec

Using Zeftron solution dyed nylon and “Computer Yarn Placement”, individual yarns are digitally tufted with incredible detail and intricate patterns with detail down to one pixel per end. Colortec allows the designer to use up to 6 colors. This “cut-pile” product simulates the look of expensive woven axminster carpets at a much lower cost.

Learn more >

Dye Injection

With 16 colors and 625 dots per square inch, Encore can create virtually any pattern and match any color imaginable. In addition to great detail and an extensive color library, low minimums and fast lead times make our printed carpets an attractive option for your next project.
